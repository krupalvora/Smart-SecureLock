STEPS

Steps to Use our Smart Lock

  • Scan

    Tap your RFID card at the distance of 2 to 3 cm from reader.

  • Check Mail

    Check your registered email address and click on link within a minute.

  • Enter Password

    Enter your password and click on unlock button within a minute.

  • Unlock

    Congratulations you unlocked successfully.
